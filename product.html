Product Overview

GlowMind — Smart Sleep & Study Lamp

An adaptive smart lamp that automatically adjusts to support what your body needs — whether you're studying, falling asleep, or waking up refreshed.

What is GlowMind?

More Than a Lamp.
A Wellness System.

GlowMind is the world's first smart lamp designed specifically around the daily rhythm of students and young professionals. It combines adaptive LED lighting technology with behavioral health science to actively support three critical phases of your day: study, sleep, and wake-up.

Unlike traditional lamps that simply light a room, GlowMind understands context. It responds to your schedule, tracks your habits, and continuously optimizes your environment for maximum focus, rest, and energy.

✓
Adaptive LED technology with full spectrum color control
✓
Integrated mobile app for scheduling and habit tracking
✓
Built-in soft audio for sleep and focus sessions
✓
Energy-efficient design with long LED lifespan

Expected Price: $39.00  ·  Perfect for Students, Families & Office Workers

Core Functionality

Three Intelligent Modes

Each mode is engineered to support a specific phase of your daily rhythm.

📚
Focus Mode

Stay Concentrated

Cool light automatically adjusts brightness and color temperature to help you stay concentrated and protect your eyes during long study sessions.

🌙
Sleep Mode

Relax & Wind Down

At your scheduled bedtime, GlowMind's light slowly dims to warm, amber tones while soft ambient sounds begin playing. This gentle combination signals your brain to begin producing melatonin, helping you fall asleep naturally and faster.

☀️
Wake-Up Light

Natural Wake-Up

Gradually brightens in the morning for a natural wake-up — no jarring alarms. Your body rises gently, energized, and ready to perform at its best.

GlowMind App

Your Wellness Dashboard

GlowMind connects to the NexSleep app — putting you in full control of your lighting environment and tracking your wellness over time.

💡 Light Control

Adjust brightness, color temperature, and mode from anywhere via the app.

⏰ Smart Schedules

Set recurring study blocks and sleep schedules that GlowMind follows automatically.

📊 History & Trends

Track your study hours and sleep duration over days, weeks, and months.

🎯 Habit Insights

Personalized suggestions to help you build healthier, more consistent daily routines.
