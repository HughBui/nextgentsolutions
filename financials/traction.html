Development Milestones

What We've Built So Far

Q3 2025

Concept & Research Phase

Completed initial market research, validated core problem through student surveys, and defined the three-mode product concept based on circadian lighting science.

Q4 2025

Product Design & Prototyping

Developed initial product specifications, designed the lamp form factor, and completed first hardware and app wireframes. Team fully assembled.

Q1 2026

Prototype Testing

Conducted structured testing sessions with target users — students aged 15–22. Gathered feedback on all three modes, app usability, and willingness to pay.

Q2 2026 (Current)

Investor Fundraising

Preparing for seed round. Business proposal complete. Partnership discussions underway with student organization networks and distribution channels.

Q3–Q4 2026

Launch Readiness (Post-Funding)

Upon funding close: finalize manufacturing partnerships, complete app development, execute marketing launch campaign, begin first sales.
