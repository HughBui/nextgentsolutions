What GlowMind Can Do

From adaptive brightness to behavioral tracking — every GlowMind feature is designed with a clear purpose: to help you focus better, sleep deeper, and live healthier.

Core Features

Engineered for Your Wellbeing

Seven key capabilities that set GlowMind apart from any ordinary lamp on the market.

🔆

Adaptive Brightness

Automatically adjusts light intensity based on the current mode and time of day. Bright and focused for studying, progressively softer toward bedtime, and gradually brightening at wake-up.

🌡️

Circadian Lighting

Uses color temperature science to support your body's natural circadian rhythm. Cool white light boosts alertness during study; warm amber light in the evening promotes natural melatonin production.

🎵

Built-In Audio Guidance

Integrated speakers play curated ambient sounds during Sleep Mode — gentle rain, soft white noise, or calming music — to help the mind relax and transition naturally into sleep.

📱

Full App Control

The GlowMind companion app gives you complete control over every lamp setting, mode, schedule, and preference — all from your smartphone, even remotely.

⏱️

Pomodoro Timer

Built-in Pomodoro technique support divides study time into focused intervals with short breaks. GlowMind adjusts lighting cues to signal work and rest phases automatically.

⚡

Energy Saving Mode

Smart occupancy and schedule detection automatically reduces power consumption when the lamp is not actively needed — extending LED lifespan and lowering energy costs.

🎨

Mood Lighting

Beyond the three core modes, users can select custom color scenes for relaxation, creativity, or personal preference — turning GlowMind into a versatile ambient light for any situation.

📊

Habit & History Tracking

The app logs every study session and sleep cycle, displaying trends and patterns over time to help users understand and improve their daily wellness habits.

🔔

Smart Schedule Alerts

Set intelligent reminders for study start times, break intervals, and wind-down routines. GlowMind gently prompts you through your day with lighting and app notifications.

App Features

Your Daily Wellness Dashboard

The GlowMind mobile app is the control center for your entire wellness routine. It's designed to be intuitive, powerful, and genuinely useful — not just a remote control, but a true health companion.

Morning

Wake-Up Mode Activation

Lamp brightens automatically at your scheduled time. App shows today's study schedule and energy score.

Study Time

Study Mode + Pomodoro

Adaptive brightness activates. Timer begins. Break reminders keep you fresh and focused throughout long sessions.

Evening

Wind-Down Sequence

App sends a gentle reminder to begin winding down. Lamp transitions to warm tones automatically.

Bedtime

Sleep Mode + Audio

Lamp dims fully as ambient sounds begin. App logs sleep start time for tomorrow's history report.
