No existing product combines adaptive study lighting, circadian sleep support, and behavioral health tracking in one affordable, student-focused device. That gap is our opportunity.

The Landscape

Understanding the Competition

GlowMind competes across three categories — each with significant gaps that GlowMind uniquely fills.

🕯️

Traditional Lamps

Standard desk lamps with fixed brightness and no smart features. Low cost but zero health intelligence. They light a room — they don't support a person.

No Smart Features
💡

Premium Smart Lamps

Products like Philips Hue offer color and brightness control via app — but at high price points, requiring ecosystem lock-in, with no student wellness focus or sleep guidance.

High Price, No Health Focus
😴

Sleep Apps / Devices

Sleep tracking apps and white noise machines address sleep in isolation — but they don't integrate with a study workflow, and they offer no adaptive lighting component.

Partial Solution Only
