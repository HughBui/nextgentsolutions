About NextGen Solutions

We are a student-founded technology startup on a mission to build smarter products that improve how people study, sleep, and live — starting with GlowMind.

Our Story

Born from a Real Problem

NextGen Solutions (NGS) was born from a simple but powerful insight: millions of students around the world study under the wrong light, sleep poorly, and wake up drained — not because they lack discipline, but because their environment works against them.

Our founding team experienced this firsthand. Late-night study sessions under harsh fluorescent lights, eyes burning, struggling to fall asleep after hours of screen exposure. We knew technology had the power to fix this.

So we asked: What if your lamp could actually understand you? What if it could protect your eyes while you study, ease you into sleep at night, and wake you up gently each morning? That question became GlowMind.
